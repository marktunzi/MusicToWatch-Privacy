    <h2>1. Who we are</h2>
    <p>Music To Watch is an iPhone and Apple Watch app that lets you import your own audio files, sync them to Apple Watch, and listen offline.</p>

    <h2>2. Data we collect</h2>
    <p><strong>We do not collect personal data through our own servers in the current version of the app.</strong></p>
    <p>This means Music To Watch does not require account registration and does not upload your audio files to our servers.</p>

    <h2>3. How the app works with your data</h2>
    <p>The app may access the following on your device only to provide core functionality:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Files you choose to import</strong> (for example audio files selected from the Files app)</li>
      <li><strong>Local app storage</strong> to save imported audio and app data (such as track and playlist information)</li>
      <li><strong>Apple Watch connectivity</strong> to transfer selected audio from your iPhone to your Apple Watch</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Your imported audio is processed locally on your devices for import, storage, sync, and playback.</p>

    <h2>4. Analytics, advertising, and tracking</h2>
    <p>Music To Watch does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.</p>
    <p>In the current version, Music To Watch does not use third-party analytics services to collect usage data.</p>

    <h2>5. Data sharing</h2>
    <p>We do not sell your personal data.</p>
    <p>We do not share your imported audio files with third parties through our own servers.</p>

    <h2>6. Data retention and deletion</h2>
    <p>Audio files and related app data are stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch (when synced).</p>
    <p>You can remove audio files from the app by deleting them in the app interface (where available) or by removing the app from your device.</p>

    <h2>7. Children’s privacy</h2>
    <p>Music To Watch is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers.</p>

    <h2>8. Changes to this Privacy Policy</h2>
    <p>We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app updates, new features, or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised “Last updated” date.</p>
